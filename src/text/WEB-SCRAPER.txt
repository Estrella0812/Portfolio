- Description - </br>
Python-based solution designed to automate the process 
of collecting information required by the company through
numbers of websites which is then collected and organized
into an excel sheet.</br> </br>
- Motivation - </br>
During my involvement in the VFC intrapreneurship, 
I was faced with a laborious task of individualy copy-pasting
information about every estate agents in a city. 
Driven by the desire to optimize my time, I began on 
developing an automated solution.
